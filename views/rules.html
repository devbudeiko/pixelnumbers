Pixel Numbers

Game Overview and Rules

Pixel Numbers is a game of luck where two players take turns tapping a button with numbers, aiming to score more points than their opponent.

Each player takes turns tapping the button with numbers. After each tap, a random number between 1 and 100 is generated. The first player starts the game by making the first tap, then the turn passes to the second player. The game ends after both players have made one tap. The player with the higher score wins.

Withdrawal of funds

Funds can be withdrawn in two sections:
• Withdrawal - payouts for games
• Referrals - payouts for referrals

When you click the Withdraw button, a payout request is created. After the request is submitted, the funds will be credited to your wallet (which you connected in the app) within 24 hours.
